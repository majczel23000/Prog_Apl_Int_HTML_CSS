About me

Michał

FRONT-END DEVELOPER
My name is Michał. I am interested in front end development. I like to be up-to-date, that's why I check the news introduced into the web development world every day. I know from experience that the front-end is not as easy as it might seem and that is why while working with each project, I try to meet the expectations and create the highest quality software. Apart from job, my hobbies are cycling, exercising and DIY in the house.
